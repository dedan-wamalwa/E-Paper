THE DAILY DEDAN

Date : April 8th 2020

The Daily Dedan newspaper today brings you top stories in the country and the world including the global pandemic, Coronavirus.
We also bring you the twist of matters as the WHO now changes tune and encourages individuals to wear masks.
They had earlier on warned that wearing masks could lead to the spread of the virus. Citizens have been advised to wear
masks when they visit social places as they continue observing other measures to prevent the spread of the
virus including frequent hand-washing,sanitizing and keeping social distance.
